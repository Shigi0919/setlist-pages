Privacy Policy

Setlist

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the iOS app "Setlist" (hereinafter "the App") handles personal information.

Information We Collect

The developer of this App does not collect any personal information from users.

All data created within the App, including setlists and song data, is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Advertising

This App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may automatically collect the following information for ad delivery and optimization:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage information

For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

If you purchase the ad removal option via in-app purchase, all advertising and associated data collection will cease.

Music Library Access

This App may request access to your Music Library to retrieve song information (title, artist name, and duration). This information is used solely for registering songs within the App and is never transmitted externally.

In-App Purchases

This App offers in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit. All payment processing is managed by Apple, and the developer has no access to your credit card or other payment information.

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any significant changes will be communicated through this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us through the App Store listing page.
