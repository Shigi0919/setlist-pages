Privacy Policy

Setlist

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Setlist" app (iOS and Android versions, hereinafter "the App") handles personal information.

Information We Collect

The developer of this App does not collect any personal information from users.

All data created within the App, including setlists and song data, is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Advertising

This App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may automatically collect the following information for ad delivery and optimization:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage information

For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

If you purchase the ad removal option via in-app purchase, all advertising and associated data collection will cease.

Usage Analytics

This App uses PostHog for anonymous event analytics to understand feature usage and improve the App. The information collected includes:

  • Feature usage patterns (which features are used and how often)
  • App launch and screen navigation data

This information is collected in a non-personally identifiable manner and is used solely for improving the App. For more details, please refer to PostHog's Privacy Policy.

Music Library Access

This App may request access to the music and audio files on your device (the Music Library on iOS, or on-device audio files on Android) to retrieve song information (title, artist name, and duration). This information is used solely for registering songs within the App and is never transmitted externally.

In-App Purchases

This App offers an in-app purchase (ad removal). Payment processing is handled by each platform's billing system (Apple's StoreKit on iOS, and Google Play's billing system on Android), and the developer has no access to your credit card or other payment information.

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any significant changes will be communicated through this page.

Data Deletion

This App does not require account registration, and analytics data (PostHog) is collected in an anonymous, non-personally identifiable form. If you would like the analytics data we have collected to be deleted, please contact us at the address below and we will take care of it. Advertising-related data (advertising ID) can be reset or deleted from your device settings.

Setlist and song data stored on your device is never transmitted externally and can be deleted by you at any time by uninstalling the App or through in-app actions.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, or to request data deletion, please contact us at the address below.

Email: shigi.studio.app.support@gmail.com
